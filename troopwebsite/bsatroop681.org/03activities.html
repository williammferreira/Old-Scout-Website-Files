TROOP 681 ACTIVITIES AND PICTURES
Troop 681 is an active, outdoor-oriented Troop, with at least one major outdoor activity every month. The Troop has day activities through the year.

We offer programs tailored to age group, and we are committed to bringing the Scouts along through a series of progressively challenging activities.

Every summer the Troop sponsors a week at Boy Scout camp, and we are proud to be able to use funds from our annual fundraiser to help subsidize the camping expenses for the boys.
