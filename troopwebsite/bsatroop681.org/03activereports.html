RECENT TROOP ACTIVITIES
BACKPACKING!!! 2015!!!

In March, different patrols went to different hiking trails around Virginia to test their outdoorsman skills. The kids had a great time and discovered a new kind of fun.

JANUARY 2015 Freeze-o-ree!
In January, Troop 681 went on an overnight camp from the 10-11 out in sub-freezing temperatures! They went to Prince William Forest and focused on orienteering and Scoutcraft training. All people there had a blast!
DECEMBER 2014 XMAS TREE SALES!
This past December, the entire troop was cheerful as they helped sell Christmas trees to local families. Every weekday from 6-9 p.m. and every Saturday and Sunday from 9 a.m.-9 p.m. Scout spirit was shared with all, through rain (snow) and sun.
